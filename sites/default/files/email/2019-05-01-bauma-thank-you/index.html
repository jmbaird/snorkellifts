Snorkel Celebrated 60 Years at Bauma 2019

We would like to sincerely thank you for visiting Snorkel at Bauma 2019, and for being part of our week-long celebration in honor of 60 years in business.

To mark our 60th anniversary, Snorkel debuted new products along with the global production of popular models. The new Snorkel S3019E unveiled an industry-first sunken scissor stack, featuring an ANSI compliant design that fits through standard doorways.

Bauma also marked the official launch of the new Snorkel SR1442 and SR1745 rough terrain telehandlers into the European market. Snorkel also launched four models into global production, including the electric-drive Snorkel TM12E and TM16E telescopic mast lifts, the 460SJ and 660SJ mid-size telescopic boom lifts as well as the S3215L lightweight electric scissor lift.
