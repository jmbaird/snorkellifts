We hope that you can join us in Munich from 8 – 14 April to celebrate Snorkel’s 60th anniversary at Bauma 2019.

With our largest stand to date, here’s a quick preview of what you can expect from Snorkel at the show:

NEW PRODUCTS

Celebrating 60 years of innovation, Snorkel will be globally launching the revolutionary, patent-pending Snorkel S3019E electric drive scissor lift. Snorkel will be taking orders at the show for this new model, which will enter production in Q4 2019.

In addition, Snorkel will be showcasing its expanded line of rough terrain telehandlers with the new Snorkel SR1442 and SR1745 models, plus displaying CE production models of the Snorkel TM16E, S3215L, 460SJ and 660SJ, all of which are now available to order.

NO BETTER GIFT THAN A LIFT!

Invite your customers to visit our booth to enter our prize draw for a chance to WIN a Snorkel ML24C material lift*.

*Terms and conditions apply. Ask for details. The winner will be drawn at 6.30pm on Friday, April 12, 2019.

LEGENDARY HOSPITALITY

Snorkel has garnered quite the reputation for its now-famous Bauma BBQ lunch, which returns to Bauma 2019 from 11.30am – 2.30pm daily. Snorkel will also be serving up Munich Mules at its Sundowner Cocktails event on its VIP deck from 6.30pm – 8.00pm on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday and Friday evenings after the show.

New for 2019, we have also introduced a barista coffee bar on the ground level of our booth.

Watch for your Snorkel VIP pass arriving in the mail shortly, giving you and your customers access to all of the complimentary hospitality.

A PARTY LIKE NO OTHER

Wednesday, April 10th from 6.30pm – 10.00pm

Snorkel is firing up for a spectacular 'fire & ice' themed evening to celebrate its 60th anniversary at its Bauma stand. An exclusive invite-only event, this promises to be a night to remember! Look out for your invitation arriving in the mail soon, and don’t forget to RSVP!

Find Snorkel at Stand FS.1002/3 in the Outdoor Area.
